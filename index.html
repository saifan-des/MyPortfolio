Process

How I work.

Step 01

Discovery & Understanding

Before writing a single line of code, I take time to understand your goals, your audience, and the problem you're solving. Clear requirements make great outcomes.

Step 02

Planning & Architecture

I map out the structure, decide on the right tech stack, and plan the component architecture. Good planning saves time and prevents costly rewrites later.

Step 03

Design & Development

I build iteratively — starting with a solid foundation and refining as I go. Regular check-ins keep you informed and in control throughout the build.

Step 04

Review & Refinement

Feedback loops are essential. I welcome revisions and iterate until the result genuinely matches your vision — no compromises on quality.

Step 05

Launch & Handoff

Clean handoff with documentation, optimized assets, and deployment support. I make sure you're confident managing and growing what we've built together.
